ABOUT ME

HELLO!

As a passionate and versatile graphic designer, I thrive in the intersection of creativity and technology. With a background in Mass Media and Communication, my expertise spans across motion graphics, digital marketing, and interactive media design. My design journey is deeply rooted in storytelling, where each project becomes an opportunity to craft visually compelling narratives that resonate with audiences.

Currently, I am pursuing an MSc in Interactive Digital Media at Trinity College Dublin, driven by my desire to create user-centered designs that address real-world challenges. I am proficient in Adobe Creative Suite and enjoy working on branding, social media content creation, and video editing. I believe that design has the power to inform, inspire, and transform, and I am excited to continue exploring this dynamic field.
